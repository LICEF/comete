CERES is in the wake of the Semantic Web and is based on the new ISO-MLR (Metadata for learning resources) and adopts the RDF (Resource Description Framework) standard approach.

This open source software is a variation of the COMETE tool developed under the BRER project and implemented by the Centre for Research LICEF Télé-université (TÉLUQ) and the Vitrine technologie-éducation (VTE) of the Collège de Bois-de-Boulogne, with the collaboration of ORI-OAI project (France) and GTN-Québec.
